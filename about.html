About Us

Newpage Educational Consult was founded in 2019, for 3 years has provided qualified, verified and competent tutors to various clients in Nigeria and abroad. Newpage Educational Consult has built a legacy of excellence, affordable home coaching, provide learning and academic alternatives and offer a tailored learning program both in-person and online and has been an instrumental in providing top range tutors. Our focus is linking learners with qualified tutors.
